Consulting

Aja works with founders and executive teams who need to understand how AI is reshaping discovery—and make the right strategic bets before the window closes.

Her focus is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO): how brands show up in AI surfaces like ChatGPT and Google AI Mode. At HubSpot, Aja designed and implemented the playbook that has made HubSpot the most visible in its category.

How It Works

Engagements are structured as ongoing strategy conversations.

Clients book a 30–60 minute call each month to:

  • Pressure-test their AEO strategy and roadmap
  • Sense-check priorities, resourcing, and sequencing
  • Get early insight into what's working (and breaking) across AI search
  • Translate theory into decisions their teams can execute

Who This Is For

  • Series B–C SaaS founders and CMOs navigating post-SEO growth
  • Growth and content leaders responsible for organic acquisition in an AI-first world
  • Teams with strong SEO foundations who need to evolve (quickly).

Consulting is offered on a highly limited, retainer-style basis. If you're interested, reach out with a short note on your company, stage, and what you're trying to solve.
